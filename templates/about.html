About Despair Change

Despair Change, emerged from a well intending pledge I didn’t follow through on. Inspired by the 'Douchebag Jar' from the TV show, New Girl (see video below), I said I would donate a dollar to the ACLU every time something in the news made me feel a sense of despair, but I never did it, because the donation form on the ACLU’s website was long and set up in a way that made me feel like the effort wasn’t worth it for just a dollar. If there had been a Paypal button, and a way for me to see that that single dollar would make a meaningful impact, I would have donated.

Our goal is to facilitate microdonations by people who want to make a positive impact during difficult moments.
